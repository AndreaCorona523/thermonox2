Functioning

The ThermoNox® system is based on the principle that food pests such as e.g. eg moths, flour beetle, as well as house pests such as e.g. eg Woodworms, bedbugs, mites, clothes moths including their development stages and eggs are safely killed at a temperature of approx. 50 °C for protein coagulation.

FOR AN EFFECTIVE DISINFECTION, YOU MUST ALWAYS MEET TWO BASIC REQUIREMENTS:

On the one hand, the pest eradication temperature must reach 50 °C – 60 °C and remain constant for long enough, even in corners, holes and also inside machinery.

On the other hand, the indicated temperature must not be exceeded in any case, otherwise the implementation of the system would not be suitable due to the damage that excess heat could cause.

THE THERMONOX® SYSTEM IS CLEARLY DIFFERENT FROM OTHER HEAT EXTERMINATION SYSTEMS BY SOLVING THIS PROBLEM IN AN EASY AND EFFECTIVE WAY:

❮

Electric heaters in rooms with danger of dust explosion

Our ThermoNox® disinfection heaters can be introduced directly into the buildings to be disinfected, including also rooms with danger of dust explosion since the maximum temperature is limited inside the ThermoNox® heaters. Our system is flexible and adapts to any building or room.

Progressive and slow heating

Through the slow heating of the air, a more profitable energy use is achieved and possible expansion damage caused by heat is thus avoided.

Air circulation

The heating of the air in the room is achieved through the circulation of air inside the building. The powerful fans incorporated in the heaters eliminate cold air currents and thus guarantee optimal heat distribution. All gaps are reached as heat retention is achieved over a long distance through air circulation, even behind heavy installations and hard-to-reach cracks.

Thermal regulation

Temperature is directly controlled by 3 thermostats built into each ThermoNox® heater that automatically turn off the heaters when the room temperature is above 50°C and turn them back on when it drops below. This on and off system is also known as timed. Through this exact temperature control, damage that could be caused by heat is avoided and even highly sensitive electronic equipment and modern electrical installations can be disinfected.

The air in the room

Unlike other thermal systems, which heat cold outside air and inject it into the building (“pipe method”), the ThermoNox® system only heats the room air in a closed system.
