ThermoNox®

The ThermoNox® heat treatment for completely non-toxic disinsection was developed in 1994, being the practical application of heat technology a fundamental pillar. From the beginning of the development of ThermoNox®, the close cooperation with the German professional food and catering mutual “Berufsgenossenschaft Nahrungsmittel und Gaststätten” as well as with a leading insurance company was decisive.

The safety and high degree of maturity of the ThermoNox® system have always been top priority.

In 1997, the limited company ThermoNox® GmbH was founded, which today is represented by the managing directors Hans and Martin Hofmeir.

Since then, the development, sale and production of heat treatments have been closely linked to guarantee maximum efficiency in any situation. Although the system has been tested and has proven to be totally effective, it will always depend on the degree of customer involvement.

We work with very modern and environmentally friendly technology and strive to maintain the most modern technological standard. In addition, we especially value direct contact with the client in order to adapt our services perfectly to their practical needs and to meet the expectations of the clients as best as possible.

The most important thing is that our customers are satisfied!

The worldwide growing demand for ThermoNox® shows us that we are not on the wrong track.

We offer the following services:

  • Advice and customer service
  • Carrying out heat treatments in large spaces
  • Rental and sale to pest control companies and final customers
  • Training and workshops around the world
